Welcome to the CSC 582 Project Exhibit!

CSC 582: Computational Models of Interactive Narrative is a masters-level course in the Computer Science Department at NC State University, taught by Chris Martens in the Fall 2020 semester.

These works were created by the students in the class for their final projects. Most of them are interactive narrative works made with Twine or Inform 7; all are playable on the web through the links below. Students were also asked to provide a short video trailer for each project. Please take a look around!

If you're here on Friday, November 20, between 5-8pm US Eastern Time, please visit our gather.town for a real-time exhibit opening party! (Link will be added here when it's ready!) Some of the projects' creators will be there to say hello.
