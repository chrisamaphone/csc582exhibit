Welcome to the CSC 582 Project Exhibit!

CSC 582: Computational Models of Interactive Narrative is a masters-level course in the Computer Science Department at NC State University, taught by Chris Martens.

These works were created by the students in the class for their final projects, shared with the public with consent! Please take a look around!

If you're here on Friday, November 20, between 5-8pm US Eastern Time, please visit our gather.town for a real-time exhibit opening party! Some of the projects' creators will be there to say hello.
